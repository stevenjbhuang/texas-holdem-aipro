What animation feel are you going for?

This shapes timing, easing, and how much the game "pauses" for animations.

A

Snappy & Responsive

Short animations (100–200ms). Cards slide in fast, chips pop to the pot instantly. Game never feels sluggish. Best when players want a quick pace.

Examples: PokerStars fast mode, Zynga Poker

B

Cinematic & Dramatic

Longer animations (300–600ms) with easing. Cards arc from dealer to players, chips cascade into the pot, winners get a spotlight moment. Feels premium.

Examples: GGPoker, WSOP app, casino-style apps

C

Balanced (Recommended)

200–350ms with ease-out curves. Animations are noticeable and satisfying but never block the flow. AI think time hides most animation overhead.

Examples: 888poker, PartyPoker
