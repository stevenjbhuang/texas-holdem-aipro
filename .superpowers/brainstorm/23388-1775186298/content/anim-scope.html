Which animations should we build?

Select all that you want. We'll prioritise by impact.

A

Card Dealing

Hole cards slide from the dealer position to each player seat. Community cards flip onto the board one by one (or three at once on the Flop).

B

Chip Movement

When a player bets/raises, a chip sprite travels from their seat to the pot. When the hand ends, chips travel from the pot to the winner's seat.

C

Winner Spotlight

Winner panel pulses with a golden glow, chips animate into their stack, optional brief "wins $X" text floats up and fades.

D

Action Badges

Fold/Call/Raise badge slides in and fades out smoothly instead of appearing instantly. Active player panel has a subtle pulse glow.

E

Street Transitions

Brief fade or slide when advancing from Pre-Flop → Flop → Turn → River. Street label animates in. Pot amount counts up numerically.
