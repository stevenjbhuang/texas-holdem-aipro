# Texas Hold'em Rules

You are playing No-Limit Texas Hold'em poker. Here are the rules:

## The Deal
- Each player receives 2 private hole cards (only you can see yours).
- 5 community cards are dealt face-up over three rounds: Flop (3 cards), Turn (1 card), River (1 card).
- You make the best 5-card hand from any combination of your 2 hole cards and the 5 community cards.

## Hand Rankings (best to worst)
1. Royal Flush — A K Q J T of the same suit
2. Straight Flush — five consecutive cards of the same suit
3. Four of a Kind — four cards of the same rank
4. Full House — three of a kind + a pair
5. Flush — five cards of the same suit
6. Straight — five consecutive cards (mixed suits)
7. Three of a Kind — three cards of the same rank
8. Two Pair — two different pairs
9. One Pair — two cards of the same rank
10. High Card — none of the above; highest card wins

## Betting
On each street you may:
- **FOLD** — discard your hand and forfeit any chips you have bet
- **CHECK** — pass the action (only when no one has bet yet this street)
- **CALL** — match the current bet
- **RAISE** — increase the bet; the raise must be at least the size of the previous raise

Betting continues until all remaining players have put in the same amount.

## Positions
- The **dealer button** rotates clockwise each hand.
- The player left of the dealer posts the **small blind** (forced bet).
- The next player posts the **big blind** (2× small blind).
- Pre-flop, the player left of the big blind acts first.
- Post-flop, the player left of the dealer acts first.

## Winning
- If all other players fold, you win the pot immediately.
- If the hand reaches showdown, the player with the best 5-card hand wins.
- If two players tie, the pot is split equally.
